What is Digital Well-being?

Digital well-being is about using technology mindfully to enhance your life without letting it take over.

Impact of Screen Time Benefits of Mindful Usage Practical Tips Technology Boundaries Physical and Mental Health Family and Community Engagement

Impact of Screen Time

  • Physical Effects: Eye strain, headaches, and posture-related issues from prolonged use.
  • Mental Effects: Increased stress, anxiety, and decreased attention span.
  • Productivity: Overuse of screens can lead to procrastination and reduced efficiency.
  • Social Impact: Reduced in-person interactions and potential feelings of isolation.
  • Sleep Disruption: Blue light from screens can interfere with sleep patterns.

Benefits of Mindful Usage

Learn how to create balance and focus on what matters most, such as relationships and personal growth.

Practical Tips

  1. Set screen-time limits.
  2. Take regular breaks.
  3. Prioritize offline activities.

Technology Boundaries

Use features like "Do Not Disturb" and app timers to prevent overuse and reclaim your time.

Physical and Mental Health

The connection between reduced screen time can help:

  • Improved physical health:Sedentary behavior associated with excessive screen time can contribute to health issues. Reducing screen time encourages physical activity and healthier lifestyle choices.
  • Enhanced focus: Constant screen stimulation can shorten attention spans. Reducing screen time allows the brain to rest and improves concentration.
  • Reduce stress& anxiety: Social media comparisons and constant information overload can increase stress and anxiety. Limiting screen time can help lower stress hormones.

Family and Community Engagement

Encourage shared activities that strengthen bonds and reduce unnecessary device use during quality time.
