Frequently Asked Questions

How does technology affect mental health? +
Technology can impact mental health by increasing stress, anxiety, or sleep disruption if overused. However, it can also provide tools for mindfulness and self-care when used responsibly.
How can I reduce screen time? +
You can reduce screen time by setting limits, using focus apps, and scheduling offline activities.
How can I use technology mindfully? +
Mindful technology use involves setting boundaries, avoiding multitasking, and being intentional about how and when you use your devices.
How can I encourage Digital Well-being in my family? +
Encourage open communication, set device-free zones, and lead by example to promote healthy habits.
What are the signs of digital addiction? +
Signs of digital addiction include constant checking of devices, reduced face-to-face interactions, and feeling anxious when offline.
What tools or apps can help improve digital well-being? +
Tools like screen time trackers, focus apps, and guided meditation apps can help you manage your digital habits effectively.
How can I maintain a healthy balance between work and technology? +
Set work hours, take breaks from screens, and avoid using work devices during personal time to maintain a healthy work-life balance.
